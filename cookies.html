mypropertymate.app uses minimal storage technologies. We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking. This page explains exactly what we store and why.

What we use instead of cookies

mypropertymate.app primarily uses browser localStorage rather than traditional HTTP cookies. localStorage stores data directly on your device and is never automatically sent to our servers with each request, unlike cookies.

We may also use sessionStorage for temporary in-session state. This is cleared automatically when you close your browser tab.

Traditional cookies

We do not set any traditional HTTP cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. Our hosting infrastructure (Netlify) may set technical cookies required for CDN and security functions. These are outside our control and governed by Netlify's own privacy and cookie policies.
